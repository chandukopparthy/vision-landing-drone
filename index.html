How Confidence is Calculated:

The landing confidence is a composite index updated at 1Hz based on four streams: obstacles detected by computer vision subtract 70% from baseline; high wind speeds subtract up to 50% relative to safety parameters; battery capacity below 20% drops general reliability by 40%. The landing autopilot overrides automatically if total safety rating dips below 50%.

Perceptual Modality Descriptions

VISION Modality Processes raw camera feeds to detect bounding boxes of objects. If obstacles like forklifts or personnel are inside the envelope, the landing pad is flagged unsafe, and a heavy confidence penalty is enforced to prevent collision.
ENVIRONMENTAL Modality Monitors localized ambient atmospheric parameters, primarily wind speed. Wind speeds exceeding 15 knots introduce extreme drift risk, resulting in automatic aborts and holding patterns until conditions settle.
INTEROCEPTION Modality Tracks the internal state and system health of the drone hardware. Low battery charge levels (< 20%) mandate emergency overrides, forcing landing gear deployment and initiating immediate touchdown commands.
SPATIAL Modality Monitors precise location telemetry coordinates (Latitude, Longitude, Altitude, heading, and speed). Guides vertical descent speed constraints to confirm structural landing deceleration limits.
